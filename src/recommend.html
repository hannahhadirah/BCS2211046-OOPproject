TOP 5 HIKING DESTINATIONS IN MALAYSIA

  1. Mount Kinabalu, Sabah

    Best high-altitude multi-day hike 8.5km (5.25 miles), 2 days, easy to moderate

    Choose a hiking trail that matches your fitness level and experience. Research the trail length, elevation gain, terrain, and difficulty to ensure it's suitable for you.

  2. Penang Hill, George Town

    Best urban hike 5.8km (3.6 miles) round trip, 4–6 hours, moderate

    Mention Penang, and most will think of the street art, hip cafes and colonial architecture of its capital George Town, not the forested hills stretching from the town limits to the northwestern corner of the island. This natural wonderland covers 12,481 hectares of marine and terrestrial ecosystems, but most visitors see just the small section around Penang Hill – colonial Malaya’s first hill station, famed for its views over the Straits Sea and the Malaysian mainland.

  3. Bukit Tabur, Selangor

    Thrilling Ridge Hike

    Embark on an adrenaline-pumping adventure along the razor-sharp ridges of Bukit Tabur. This challenging hike demands concentration and agility as you navigate steep inclines and rocky outcrops. With stunning vistas of the Klang Gates Quartz Ridge and surrounding landscapes, it's a favorite among experienced hikers seeking an exhilarating day out near Kuala Lumpur.

  4. Broga Hill, Semenyih

    Short, Scenic Hike, The 2.3-kilometer (1.4 miles) trail

    Perfect for beginners and those short on time, Broga Hill offers a relatively easy ascent with rewarding panoramic views. Consists of gentle slopes and stairs, making it accessible to all fitness levels. Witness a breathtaking sunrise or sunset from the summit, making it a popular spot for outdoor photography and relaxation.

  5. Cameron Highlands Trails, Pahang

    Scenic Day Hikes

    Explore the lush landscapes and rolling hills of Cameron Highlands through various day hikes of different lengths and difficulties. Trails range from easy strolls to more challenging treks, offering picturesque views of tea plantations, mossy forests, and waterfalls. Ideal for families and nature enthusiasts seeking a day of adventure amidst cool mountain air.


TOP 5 HIKING DESTINATIONS OVERSEAS

  1. Torres del Paine National Park, Chile

    Iconic Patagonian Trek

    A multi-day adventure through the breathtaking landscapes of Torres del Paine National Park awaits. Choose from various routes catering to different fitness levels and durations, offering opportunities to witness towering peaks, glacial lakes, and abundant wildlife. Research the trail options and prepare accordingly for an unforgettable journey in the heart of Patagonia.

  2. Tongariro Alpine Crossing, New Zealand

    Volcanic Wonderland

    Experience the otherworldly landscapes of Tongariro National Park on a thrilling day hike. Trek through volcanic terrain, past steaming vents, and vibrant crater lakes, all while enjoying panoramic views of the North Island's central plateau. Research the trail conditions and weather forecasts, and pack accordingly for an adventure in one of New Zealand's most iconic natural wonders.

  3. Grand Canyon Rim-to-Rim, USA

    Epic Canyon Adventure

    Challenge yourself with a rim-to-rim hike through the awe-inspiring Grand Canyon. This strenuous trek offers unparalleled views of the canyon's geological wonders, from towering cliffs to the meandering Colorado River below. Research the trail conditions and logistics, and prepare for a once-in-a-lifetime experience in one of America's most iconic national parks.

  4. Mount Kilimanjaro, Tanzania

    Roof of Africa Expedition

    Summit Africa's highest peak on an unforgettable trek up Mount Kilimanjaro. Choose from various routes, each offering unique landscapes and challenges, as you ascend through diverse ecosystems to Uhuru Peak. Research the route options, altitude acclimatization strategies, and necessary gear to ensure a safe and successful climb to the "Roof of Africa."

  5. Annapurna Circuit, Nepal

    Himalayan Odyssey

    Embark on a legendary trek around the Annapurna Massif in the heart of the Nepalese Himalayas. This classic trek offers unparalleled views of snow-capped peaks, terraced hillsides, and traditional mountain villages. Research the trekking routes, altitude acclimatization strategies, and cultural highlights, and prepare for an adventure of a lifetime in one of the world's most spectacular mountain ranges.
